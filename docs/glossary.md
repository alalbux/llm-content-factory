# Glossary

**Campaign**  
A high-level content initiative with a goal and audience.

**Content Task**  
A single piece of work derived from a campaign.

**Content Version**  
A generated or edited version of content with metadata.

**LLM Planner**  
LLM role responsible for planning tasks.

**LLM Generator**  
LLM role responsible for generating drafts.

**LLM Critic**  
LLM role responsible for reviewing quality and risk.

**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**  
Technique to ground LLM output in external knowledge.

**Worker**  
Background service that executes async jobs.

**CMS**  
System of record for editorial content.
